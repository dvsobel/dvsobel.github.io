A little bit about myself...

I'm Danielle Sobel and I'm passionate about improving the lives of others through design. I love meeting people who are motivated and unafraid of sharing their ideas with the world, and am constantly learning. I studied painting and art history in my undergraduate studies at Penn State University and graduated in 2014. Before receiving my MFA in Design and Technology from Parsons, The New School (With Honors) in 2018, I worked in Tel Aviv as a designer for a tech startup.

I am currently a Senior User Experience Designer and Researcher at Verisk Analytics, where I spend my time interfacing with business customers in identifying their needs and developing personalized solutions. I always enjoy a new challenge!

In my spare time I am an accomplished baker, and I enjoy hiking and traveling the world.
