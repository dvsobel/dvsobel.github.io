Consolidated Loss Cost Download

Password: verisk1


My Role | UX Designer & User Researcher
Tools | Axure, Sketch
Research Methods | Usability Testing and Interviews
Date | 2020








Problem Statement:
Customers stated that providing Loss Costs information via Excel or Word documents for individual state, by line of business made it difficult and inefficient to use ISO content in their daily workflow.

Our Solution:
Create a Consolidated Loss Costs Download (CLCD) product. This add-on feature in ISOnet Circulars will allow customers to obtain state-level loss cost data in one file, by line of business, for all states in which they participate. This facilitates customers' ability to integrate ISO content into their operations, and conduct country-wide analysis.






"It makes this task more doable and overtime helps me not to hire an additional person. It looks like an expense savings on our side.

-Joanne A. Attune Insuance



Validation: remote usability testing with six users

CLCD made customer workflows faster and helped them reduce errors when processing and analyzing loss costs data. The results were less manual labor, fewer errors, and more flexibility and efficiently using ISO data.
